Our Mission

EcoDIY Hub is created to encourage people to reuse waste materials and turn them into useful DIY products. Our goal is to promote creativity while supporting eco-friendly practices.

What We Offer

  • Collection of eco-friendly DIY projects
  • User-submitted creative ideas
  • Step-by-step guides for easy learning
  • Admin moderation for quality content

Why This Project?

This platform was developed as a web programming project to demonstrate frontend development, user interaction, and content management without using a database.

Future Scope

  • Add user login system
  • Upload images with projects
  • Search and filter functionality
  • Convert into full-stack application
